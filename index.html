About

I've sat in nearly every seat in engineering — from writing COBOL and device drivers to leading global organizations as a CTO. That full arc is the point: I lead technology from a foundation of having actually built it.

Over 25 years I rose from individual contributor to Senior Director reporting to the CTO, and today serve as CTO and VP of Engineering for Carrier's Automated Logic, Nlyte, Abound, and UTEC OEM businesses — a $1.1B+ portfolio with a $40M R&D budget and multidisciplinary teams across the US, India, the UK, and Romania.

Along the way I delivered a $125M, 360-developer DCIM platform in under 18 months, built an engineering center from zero to 252 people, drove on-time delivery from below 60% to above 96%, and came through five acquisitions as the person each new owner asked to stay. I'm a named inventor on multiple data center patents, including a pending Direct-to-Chip cooling system for the AI era.

Now I bring that experience to companies that need executive engineering leadership for a season — to scale, to turn delivery around, to evaluate an acquisition, or to navigate the AI-infrastructure shift — without committing to a full-time hire.
