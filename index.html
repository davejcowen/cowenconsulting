What we do

We lead the transitions that decide everything.

Companies call us for the moments where the cost of getting it wrong dwarfs the cost of getting help — a transformation, a deal, an integration, a modernization. We bring operators who have led exactly these at scale, and a structured method that turns a messy situation into a plan your team can run.

01

Digital & business transformation

Run technology as a driver of the business, not a cost center.

We've transformed the full stack of how a business runs — from ERP and core enterprise systems to data, cloud, and connected products. We align engineering investment to outcomes, modernize the operating model, and build the P&L-aware discipline that makes technology a competitive advantage.

02

M&A, divestitures & due diligence

Know what you're buying — and make the deal actually pay off.

Our principals have run technical diligence on acquisition targets, integrated five companies post-close, and carried an engineering org cleanly through a private-equity divestiture. We assess a target's real technology, team, and risk before you sign — then lead the buy-side or carve-out work that decides whether the thesis holds.

03

Engineering & support team integration

Assimilate disparate teams without losing the people or the pace.

The hardest part of any deal or reorg is the human and operational integration. We've merged engineering, QA, and support teams across acquisitions, geographies, and cultures — onto one operating model, one SDLC, and one roadmap — while keeping delivery moving and the key people in their seats.

04

Process & delivery transformation

Make delivery predictable — turn heroics into a system.

We install the requirements-gating, planning cadence, and SDLC discipline that move on-time delivery from broken to dependable — one engagement took it from below 60% to above 96%, sustained for three years. Our Engineering Org Diagnostic scores 11 domains so the plan targets what actually matters.

05

Technology & platform modernization

Move off the legacy core — without stopping the business.

We modernize architectures and platforms for where the market is heading: legacy-to-cloud, monolith-to-services, on-prem to SaaS, and the data foundation an AI roadmap needs. Deep infrastructure roots too — DCIM, building automation, and high-density cooling, including a pending Direct-to-Chip patent for AI thermal loads.

06

Fractional & interim leadership

Executive engineering leadership, for the season you need it.

When you need a seasoned hand in the seat — between CTOs, through a transition, or carrying more risk than the bench can cover — we step in as fractional or interim leadership: setting strategy, fixing delivery, and building the team that ships, then handing back something your people can run without us.

About the firm

Cowen Leadership Consulting is built on a simple idea: the hardest technology transitions should be led by people who have actually run them — not advised on them from the outside.

We're a specialist firm of senior engineering operators. We take on the work that decides whether a company's next chapter succeeds — digital transformation, M&A and divestitures, integrating disparate engineering and support teams, process transformation, and technology modernization. Every engagement pairs a seasoned operator with our structured Engineering Org Diagnostic, so the plan is grounded in evidence, not opinion, and your team can run it after we leave.

The firm was founded by Dave Cowen, who rose from writing device drivers to CTO over 25 years and today leads global engineering for a $1.1B+ portfolio with a $40M R&D budget. Along the way he delivered a $125M, 360-developer DCIM platform in under 18 months, built an engineering center from zero to 252 people, drove on-time delivery from below 60% to above 96%, and carried an engineering org through five changes of ownership — including a private-equity divestiture — as the leader each new owner asked to stay. He's a named inventor on multiple data center patents, including a pending Direct-to-Chip cooling system for the AI era.

We engage where it counts and right-size to the problem — fractional or interim leadership, an advisory cadence, or a defined transformation, diligence, or integration scope — without asking you to commit to a full-time hire.
